### Ingredients:

5-6 medium to large bananas

1 1/2 c. white flour

1 1/2 c. whole wheat flour

1 1/4 c. sugar

1/2 c. pea protein powder

2 flax eggs (2 tbsp flax meal and 6 tbsp of water or vegan milk)

3/4 c. melted butter or refined coconut oil

3 tbsp room temperature oat milk, mixed with 1 tsp white vinegar

2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp baking soda

2 tsp vanilla extract

2 tsp butter flavoring

1 tsp salt

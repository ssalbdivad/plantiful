### Ingredients:

5-6 medium to large very ripe bananas

1 1/2 c. white flour

1 1/2 c. whole wheat flour

1 1/4 c. sugar

1/2 c. pea protein powder

2 flax eggs (2 tbsp flax meal and 6 tbsp of water or vegan milk)

3/4 c. melted butter or refined coconut oil

3 tbsp room temperature oat milk, mixed with 1 tsp white vinegar("sour" milk)

2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp baking soda

2 tsp vanilla extract

2 tsp butter flavoring

1 tsp salt

### Directions:

1. Make your flax eggs by mixing the flax meal with liquid and let sit for 5-10 minutes.
2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and butter two 9 x 5 pans (8 x 4 should work fine as well)
3. In a medium bowl, mash bananas and sugar. Add vanilla, butter flavoring,
4. In a large bowl mix all dry ingredients until blended (flour, pea protein, baking soda, baking powder, salt)
5. To the large bowl, add banana mixture, "sour" milk, flax eggs and melted butter. Mix until just incorporated.
6. Evenly distribute between two pans. Bake at 350 degrees for 55-60 minutes.
